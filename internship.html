Company Background

Ampleon Philippines, Inc. is a global semiconductor manufacturing company that develops RF power solutions for industrial, communication, medical, and scientific applications. The student completed the internship under the Information Technology Department, where technology is used to support manufacturing operations, production systems, and business reporting.

Throughout the internship, the student was exposed to real-world software development, database management, enterprise applications, and technical support. This experience provided a better understanding of how information technology contributes to daily business operations in a manufacturing environment.

Problem

The Assembly Engineering and Process Control teams needed a centralized Material Consumption Report to monitor production materials more efficiently. Previously, retrieving this information required manually checking multiple sources, making the process time-consuming and difficult to analyze.

The goal of the project was to automate data collection, organize production information into a single database, and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that allows users to filter, analyze, and monitor material consumption more easily.

Release to Production

The student first reviewed the project requirements with the assigned engineer to understand the needed reports and business rules. SQL tables, stored procedures, and SQL Jobs were developed to automate the extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) of production data. After validating the data using SAP ERP and Camstar MES, the information was connected to Power BI to develop interactive dashboards. The completed report was demonstrated to users, revised based on their feedback, and prepared for Release-to-Production (R2P).

The completed Material Consumption Report allows engineers to monitor production materials using interactive filters, summarize consumed quantities, and view detailed production records. SQL Server handles the automated data processing, while Power BI provides an easy-to-use dashboard for reporting and analysis.

Learnings and Experience

My internship at Ampleon Philippines, Inc. has been one of the most valuable learning experiences in my academic journey. Aside from developing the Material Consumption Report using SQL Server and Power BI, I also gained experience in daily SAP ERP monitoring, validating production data using Camstar MES, and supporting IT Helpdesk activities. These responsibilities allowed me to experience different areas of the Information Technology department.

Working in a real manufacturing company helped me understand how software solutions directly support business operations. I learned how to communicate with engineers, gather user requirements, present system updates, and improve reports based on client feedback. Presenting my own work during meetings also improved my confidence and communication skills.

One of the biggest challenges was understanding enterprise systems and ensuring that production data remained accurate throughout the development process. However, these challenges helped the student become more independent, responsible, and confident in solving real-world technical problems.

Overall, this internship strengthened the student's technical knowledge, professional work ethics, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities. The experience has prepared the student for a future career in software development, data analytics, and enterprise information systems.
